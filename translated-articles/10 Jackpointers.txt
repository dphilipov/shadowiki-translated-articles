
'''10 Jackpointers''' is a volume of background information on ten well-known [[Shadowtalk]]s in the rules, source, and adventure/campaign volumes for the [[Shadowrun: Editions#4th Edition|fourth edition]] of [[Shadowrun]].

''In-game'' these are presented as electronic dossiers by the [[Dawkins Group]], which [[Fastjack]] uploaded to [[Jackpoint]] for member information on March 5, 2010.

"10 Jackpointers" was published by {{Cat}} on 2010-03-01 (real time) in English and exclusively as an eBook.

A translation in Brazilian Portuguese was published under the title "'''10 Plugueiros'''" by Brazilian licensee Editora New Order.

==back cover text==
===English:===
'''Everyone's Watching Everyone'''

The problem with keeping files about ne'er-do-wells is they're a curious and clever lot. Curious and clever enough to pilfer those files from the corporate compiler, repost them, and add their own commentary-providing a level of meta-information like you've never seen before. Get the scoop on [[Jackpoint]] posters like [[Kay St. Irregular]], [[Ecotope]], [[Man-of-Many-Names]], and [[Turbo Bunny]].

Remember: not everything you read is true. When the two sources of information are [[Horizon]] and Jackpoint ... all bets are off.

The Jackpointers presented in this download are: [[Baka Dabora]], [[Ecotope]], [[Fianchetto]], [[Kay St. Irregular]], [[Lei Kung]], [[Lyran]], [[Man-of-Many-Names]], [[Orbital DK]], [[Riser]], and [[Turbo Bunny]]. Each has a 2 or 3 page profile.

===Portuguese:===
'''Todo mundo vigia todo mundo'''<br/>
O problema em manter arquivos sobre plugueiros é que eles são muito inteligentes e curiosos. Curioso e inteligente o suficiente para roubar esses arquivos do compilador corporativo, republicá-los e adicionar seus próprios comentários - fornecendo um nível de meta-informação como você nunca viu antes. Receba as notícias sobre plugueiros como Kay Sta. Irregular, Ecótope, O-de-Muitos-Nomes e Cueia Nóia.

Lembre-se: nem tudo que você ler aqui é verdade. Quando as duas fontes de informação são Horizon e o Plugue ... Não confie 100%.

Os Plugueiros apresentados neste download são: Baka Dabora, Ecótope, Fianchetto, Kay Sta. Irregular, Lei Kung, Lyran, O-de-Muitos-Nomes, Orbital DK, Riser e Cueia Noia. Cada um tem um perfil de 2 ou 3 páginas.
