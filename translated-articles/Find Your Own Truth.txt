'''Find Your Own Truth''' is the third title in the regular us [[Shadowrun novel]] series, and also the third volume in the "Secrets of Power" trilogy by Robert N. Charette. The novel was published in original English by Roc Books in 1991.

A German translation was published in the same year under the title "'''Such deine eigene Wahrheit'''" as the fourth, German-language Shadowrun novel and at the same time third volume of the "Secrets of Power" trilogy by Heyne Verlag.

In 1994, Fleuve noir published a French translation of the novel under the title "''...Et trouve ta vérité!''".

In 1997, the Polish publisher ISA published the novel as a Polish translation under the title "'''Znajdź własną drogę'''.
 
In 2002, the Hungarian publishing house Beholder Kiadó Bt. published the novel in Hungarian translation under the title "'''Keresd a magad igazát'''.

In Brazil, a Portuguese translation was published under the title "'''Encontre sua Própria Verdade''' by Devir.

The Spanish publisher Timun Mas published a Spanish translation under the title "'''Enfréntate a la realidad''' on March 15, 2001."
 
On March 1, 2016, {{Cat}} published an eBook reprint of "'''Find Your Own Truth'''" as a zip file containing the ePub and mobi versions of the novel.

In 2021, Russian licensee Hobby World published a Russian translation under the title "'''Найди свою правду'''" in Russian and Cyrillic script as a hardcover.
