'''.sphinx''' is supposedly the mysterious [[gang]]leader of the [[Berlin]]s [[Matrix gang]] of "[[Hackbirds]]" in 2080.

==Matrix activities==
In October 2082, .sphinx made the statement in the [[Shadow Pulse 2082-10-25 (unofficial)|NovaPuls of 10/25/2082]] hijacked by the "Hackbirds" via [[ShadowBot]]-[[App]] and turned into the [[Shadow Pulse]], [[.chtrl]] - civic: [[Cheri Hackler]] - was '''NOT a member of the Hackbirds''', and asked that complaints about her and her behavior not be directed to the Matrix Gang. {{Ref|10}}
