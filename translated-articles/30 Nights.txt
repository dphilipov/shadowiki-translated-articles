{{Current Publication}}
'''30 Nights''' is an adventure volume for the [[Shadowrun: Editions#6th Edition|Sixth Edition]] of [[Shadowrun]]. This is set in [[Toronto]], and contains - against the backdrop of events from the setting and campaign volume "[[Cutting Black]]" - an adventure for each of the 30 nights of fatal blackouts that afflict the media metropolis and capital of [[Ontario]]-like the rest of the UCAS-and bring it to the brink of doom.

It was published in original English (and considerably late) by {{Cat}} on March 4, 2020, as an ebook in watermarked pdf format, as well as in print hardcover. In the latter, "30 Nights" includes a full-color map of [[Toronto]]s printed on the inside cover.  This is a map from the game "[[Sprawl Ops]]".

The German translation under the title "'''30 Nights and 3 Days'''' will be published in {{Pegasus V}} hardcover. The ebook pdf edition will be available from February 13, and the print version will be released for Free Roleplaying Day 2020, on March 14. It will include 3 additional adventures in the ADL influenced by events in North America.

A French translation was released on June 24, 2021 by French licensee {{Blackbooks}} under the title "'''30 Nuits'''', and includes an exclusive French addition.

==back cover text==
===English:===
NIGHT FALLS...IT WILL ONLY GET WORSE<br/>
The first night of a citywide blackout usually has rioting and lawlessness. The second night continues the chaos. The third night? Well, whoever heard about a citywide blackout going three nights?

It's about to happen, and shadowrunners caught in the middle will have to deal with more than darkness. There will be howling critters roaming free. Strange spirits and twisted metaplanar beings appearing and pursuing unknown goals. And mysterious vans that somehow still have power slowly roaming the streets, sending out some sort of signal.

The streets will be getting more dangerous, and shadowrunners will be in the middle of it, first trying to survive, then trying to earn some cash, and finally trying to uncover the secrets the blackout was supposed to hide.

30 Nights is a campaign book for Shadowrun, Sixth World. As the campaign propels players through each day of a month-long blackout, the stakes and danger accelerate toward a grand finale. Whether you pick a few days to play or dive into all 30 nights, this book will provide a Shadowrun gaming experience like no other!

===german:===
<big>'''WENN ES DUNKEL WIRD'''</big><br/>

The first night of a power outage, there is usually unrest. The second, chaos sets in. But the nights after that? How long can a power outage last? [[Runner]]s who venture into the darkness must fear for their lives: Gruesome creatures prowl the darkness, strange [[ghosts]] and twisted, metaplanar beings pursuing unknown goals. Mysterious vans drive slowly through the night, sending signals. The streets become increasingly dangerous - and events draw the Runners deeper into the blackness than they initially suspect.

30 Nights and 3 Days is a campaign book for Shadowrun 6. Thirty adventures take you through the nights of a month-long blackout, a metaplot event described in more detail in the sourcebook [[Blackout (Sourcebook)|Blackout]]. Included is a large-scale map of [[Toronto]] with all the major locations and three complete adventures in the [[ADL]]-three days related to the blackout across the ocean. With more light, but no less [[shadow]].
